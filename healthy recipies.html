Banana cake recipe

A banana cake is prepared using fresh bananas as a primary ingredient and typical cake ingredients

ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups mashed bananas
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 ⅛ cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 3 egss
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extrct
  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk

Procedure

step one

Gather all ingredients.
Preheat the oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C).
Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.

Step 2

Make cake: Mix mashed bananas with lemon juice in a small bowl.

Step 3

Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl

Step 4

Cream sugar and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy, 3 to 4 minutes.

Step 5

Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.

Step 6

Beat in flour mixture alternately with buttermilk. Stir in banana mixture.

Step 7

Pour batter into the prepared pan.

Step 8

Remove cake from the oven and place it directly into the freezer for 45 minutes.
This will make the cake very moist.

Add frost if desired
